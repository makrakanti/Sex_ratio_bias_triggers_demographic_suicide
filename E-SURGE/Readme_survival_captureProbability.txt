The .xls files are the outputs from the discussed models of adult survival and capture probability fit using E-Surge for the populations from Golem Grad Island and Konjsko. Several sheets are important: "Model definition" contains the matrices and the corresponding phrases used in the GEMACO interface of the software; "Deviance" provides the QAIC, deviance, etc.; "Reduced set of parameters" gives the parameter estimates - the variables' number coedes are defined in parentheses at first mention.
